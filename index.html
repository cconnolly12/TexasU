Courses

TIP Scholars find registering for classes easier and therefore, academic progress is enhanced. TIP reserves seats for its Scholars in highly-desired core first-year courses. Some key courses are restricted to TIP students to improve faculty to student ratio, promote the small academic community, and increase student interaction and sense of belonging. Course offerings change regularly as the University continues to determine the needs of their students. Below you can find a list of courses traditionally restricted(*) or reserving seats for TIP students.
